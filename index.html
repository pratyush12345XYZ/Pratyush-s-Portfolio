About Me

Hey there! I'm a B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering student at KIIT, Bhubaneswar. I grew up in Cuttack, Odisha, always curious about how technology could be leveraged to solve real-world problems. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion for building AI-powered tools and full-stack web applications.

I genuinely love working with data — from the gritty process of cleaning and engineering features, to analyzing trends and creating visualizations that tell a compelling story. For me, learning happens best through building. I focus on shipping fast, gathering feedback, and iterating even faster.

I've already built and deployed several real-world projects like FinRobot, Joblio, Livestock Predictor, NoZero, and am currently developing Hander-AI. I believe in writing clean code and designing interfaces that feel intuitive and premium.

When I'm not deep into code, you'll probably find me playing cricket or badminton, listening to playlists that fuel my late-night coding sessions, building random new app ideas, reading articles to hunt down the next big idea, or exploring new places.
